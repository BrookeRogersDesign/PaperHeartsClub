The Art of
Staying in Touch

I believe some things are still worth writing by hand.

In a world of instant messages and disappearing conversations, a handwritten letter asks us to slow down. To choose our words with care. To send a piece of ourselves that can't be copied or forwarded.

The Pegasus is a symbol of imagination, creativity, and connection. It reminds us that friendship isn't measured in miles, but in the moments we choose to share.

Some friends live just around the corner, while others are hundreds of miles away. The wings are for all of them.

Every letter carries more than paper. It carries the time someone set aside to think of another person, to share a story, offer encouragement, celebrate a milestone, or simply say, “I was thinking about you.” Those small gestures often become the ones we remember the longest.
